Frequently Asked Questions

I can’t attend the webinar – will a recording be available?

Yes! All of our SABF webinars are recorded, and the recordings are available at no cost. To receive a link to the recording shortly after a webinar ends, register for the webinar (even if you can’t attend it live).

What do I need to participate?

To view the webinar all you need is a device with a web browser and Internet connection. A computer or tablet or smartphone will work.

How much does the webinar/event cost?

All Score a Better Future (SABF) events are FREE

How do I register?

Under the Registration tab, enter your information and click Register. You’ll recieve an email conﬁrmation with the event details including the webinar link.
